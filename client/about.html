What We Stand For

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Excellence

We pursue the highest standards in talent discovery, event production, and participant experience, ensuring every aspect of the festival reflects quality and professionalism.

Inclusion

We believe talent has no boundaries. Everyone deserves a platform regardless of background, experience level, or location. Diversity is our strength.

Impact

We measure success by the lives we transform. From life-changing prizes to career-launching exposure, our goal is lasting positive change for every participant.

Innovation

We constantly evolve our approach to talent discovery, embracing new technologies and creative formats to deliver a world-class festival experience.

Our Journey

Key Milestones

The story of SilverVerse so far

2026 Q1

Founded

SilverVerse was established with a vision to discover and nurture emerging talent across Nigeria through a world-class festival platform.

2026 Q2

Registration Launch

Opened registration for the inaugural Voices & Visions Festival across 8 categories, attracting interest from talented individuals nationwide.

2026 Q3

Auditions & Selection

Conducted auditions across multiple zones, selecting the best performers to compete at the grand finale in Orlu.

2026 Q4

Grand Finale

The inaugural Voices & Visions Festival Grand Finale takes centre stage, showcasing the finest emerging talent from across Nigeria.

2027

Expansion

Plans underway for an expanded second edition with more categories, regional qualifiers, and bigger prizes for participants.
