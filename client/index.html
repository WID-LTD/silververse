Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the festival

What is the Voices & Visions Festival?
The Voices & Visions Festival is SilverVerse's flagship talent competition celebrating creativity, performance, and purpose-driven expression across singing, dance, spoken word, digital art, and more.
Who can participate?
The festival is open to everyone. Whether you're a performer, judge, speaker, volunteer, sponsor, spectator, or media — there's a place for you.
How much does registration cost?
Registration fees vary by ticket type: Regular (N1,000), VIP (N5,000), and VVIP/Table for Four (N10,000). Payment is processed securely via Flutterwave.
How are contestants judged?
Contestants are scored using a combined system: 60% from an expert judges panel and 40% from audience voting. This ensures both professional evaluation and public engagement.
What are the prizes?
The Grand Prize Winner receives N2,000,000+ in cash and deals. The 1st Runner Up receives N500,000 and the 2nd Runner Up receives N250,000, plus mentorship and exposure opportunities.
Where and when is the event?
The festival takes place on 1 August 2026 at Rochas Foundation, Ideato, Orlu, Imo State (opposite Kingsley Ozumba Mbadiwe University), starting at 9:00 AM Prompt.
